Our Story


Super 6 Grille House is a Chinese restaurant owned by a Chinese family, it is only a small family business run by the Lim Family in Malate, Manila started in 2005. The restaurant is considered as a commercial food service because it is a profit-oriented establishment and its main goal is to provide food and beverages to customers to create profit and give a good impression to the market. Super 6 Grille House is not a powerhouse restaurant or a big establishment, it is only a small family business .


At Super 6, they aim to give everyone a pleasurable dining experience, serving only the freshest food ranging from Filipino grill favorites - Sisig, Grilled Liempo, Buttered Chicken, Sweet and Sour Pork and many more. As of now, Super 6 has only 2 branches located at 533 Remedios Street Malate Manila and 1880 Maria Orosa Street Malate Manila which is the second branch opening soon.
